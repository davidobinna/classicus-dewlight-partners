Meet Chiugo Okechilkelu Ken-Okenini (nee Chukwu). A Counsel at Classicus & Dewlight Partners, she stands as one of the founding partners prior to the merger.

Preliminary Statement

Chiugo Okechilkelu Ken-Okenini (nee Chukwu) is a Partner at Classicus & Dewlight Partners. She is one of the founding partners of Classicus partners before the merger. Chiugo leads the Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) and Restorative Justice Unit of the firm. She has vast experience in resolution of disputes through mediation, reconciliation, negotiation and arbitration. Chiugo did her chamber attachment with Babalakin &Co . She later went under the tutelage of McCarthy& Co-Pacific Court where she further learned the ropes of the profession after being a telecommunication consultant for close to a decade. With over 18 years at the Bar and 11 years as a certified mediator with the Lagos Multi-door courthouse she has active experience in assisting clients in Nigeria and Diaspora navigate and deal with personal and business interest in different sectors like Medical and Health Law, Immigration, Business Law, Entertainment, Human rights, Family Law, Energy, Real Estate and Property management, Media, Consumer Protection, Relocation Issues. Chiugo have been involved in multiple wild range of matters, Chiugo is known for her professionalism and her ability to provide clients with tailored solutions to the most complex issues. Chiugo is part of the team developing the IPC Legal Framework in Nigeria sponsored by African Center for Disease Control through Nigeria CDC. Chiugo is an alumna of the University of Nigeria.
